Modelling Foundations

Each module is organized like a research note: motivation, mathematical object, estimator, optimization route, assumptions, diagnostics, and applied case study.

Why this course-style course exists

The Art of Modelling is designed for learners who want to read models the way researchers read systems. Regression, optimization, assumptions, and evaluation are not separate boxes. They are one argument about how data becomes evidence.

We begin with simple linear regression because it exposes the recurring anatomy of machine learning: prediction, residuals, loss functions, parameter estimation, gradients, assumptions, bias, variance, and diagnostic evidence.
